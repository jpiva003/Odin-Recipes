Huevos Rancheros (Mexico)

Description

Huevos Rancheros is a traditional Mexican breakfast dish that consists of fried eggs served on lightly fried corn tortillas, topped with a tomato-chili sauce. It is often accompanied by refried beans, Mexican-style rice, and slices of avocado or guacamole. The dish is a hearty and flavorful meal that showcases the staples of Mexican cuisine: corn, beans, and chiles.

    • Steps to make Huevos Rancheros

    1. Make Sauce: Cook chopped tomatoes, onions, and chiles until soft.
    2. Cook Tortillas: Lightly fry corn tortillas.
    3. Fry Eggs: Cook eggs sunny-side up.
    4. Assemble: Place eggs on tortillas and top with sauce. Serve with beans and avocado.
